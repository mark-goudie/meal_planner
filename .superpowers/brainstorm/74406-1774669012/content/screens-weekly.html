Interactions & Gestures

Tap a filled meal

Opens recipe detail with cooking notes, ratings, and "Start Cooking" button

Tap an empty slot

Opens recipe picker — search, browse, or "Suggest something" via AI

Long-press & drag

Rearrange meals between days. Thursday's curry can become Friday's dinner with a drag

Swipe left on a meal

Reveals "Remove" and "Swap" actions

‹ › Week navigation

Swipe horizontally or tap arrows to move between weeks

"Suggest Meals" button

AI fills empty slots based on your recipe collection, ratings, and what you haven't cooked recently. You approve each suggestion.

"Shopping List" button

Generates a combined shopping list from all planned meals, with quantities summed and items grouped by category

HTMX Implementation

Every interaction updates only the affected card via HTMX partial swap — no full page reloads. Drag-and-drop uses Alpine.js with SortableJS. The entire weekly view stays responsive and fast.

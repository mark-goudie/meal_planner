Pasta Carbonara

⏱ 25 min 👥 Serves 4 📊 Easy
Quick Italian Pasta
⭐ 5.0 from 3 cooks
Last cooked: 2 weeks ago

Ingredients

Spaghetti 400g
Guanciale 200g, cubed
Egg yolks 4 large
Pecorino Romano 100g, finely grated
Black pepper 2 tsp, freshly cracked

Steps

1 Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2 While pasta cooks, fry guanciale in a cold pan on medium heat until crispy and golden.
3 Whisk egg yolks with grated pecorino and cracked pepper in a bowl.

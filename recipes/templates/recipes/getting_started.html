Getting Started

Welcome to Meal Planner! Here’s how to make the most of your new account:

  1. Explore Example Recipes: Check out the demo recipes added to your account.
  2. Add Your Own Recipes: Use the “Add Recipe” button to save your favourites.
  3. Plan Your Meals: Create a meal plan for the week using your recipes.
  4. Generate a Shopping List: Instantly create a shopping list from your meal plan.
  5. Try AI Features: Use the AI generator to discover new recipes based on your ingredients.
  6. Review Legal & Privacy: See our Privacy Policy and Disclaimer.

If you have questions or feedback, contact us any time.
